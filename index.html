A Computational Model of a Human Neuron

Mihir Modak • September 23, 2021 • In-Class Project

Background & Motivation

This is a simulation of the activity of a single neuron using a mathematical model called the Hodgkin-Huxley Model. This model can accurately represent the excitation and the action potential of a neuron. It can also account for sodium, potassium, and chloride ion concentrations and use those parameters to drive the excitation of the neuron. In this project, I was able to implement this model in MATLAB, and successfully simulate (and control) the activity of a neuron.
